About me

Hi there and welcome! My name is Alex and I am a final year Economics and Management student at King's College London. I was born in Southwark and I was raised in a mixture of Sussex, Hampshire and Surrey. And yes, I am part asian as you can tell from the picture - I am half Thai. So I am interested in a wide variety of things ranging from fencing, kendo and ultimate frisbee. But the most important of them all is my love of collecting different kinds fo money (which i don't plan to spend). When in London, I would alwasy get excited when I would be given some change as I would never know if I were going to see a coin I had never seen before. Naturally, my collection for GBP got larger over the years to the point I would comfortably say I have about 80% of GBP designs currently in circulation. Please do check out my website and see If I am missing anything :-)
